Place Order (payments after artwork step 4)

Enter your contact, shipping and billing information for your order. After your order has been submitted a representative will review your order and contact you if we need clarification.

Approve High Quality Mock-ups**

Once our team has drafted a mockup, we will send an email with a link to view the proof. You can request changes to be made or make payments to approve your mockup.

Payments

We are able to take payments through many methods including, credit card, invoicing, cheque, or interac e-transfer.

Order is Shipped

After we finish printing (~ 10 business days standard turnaround time, rush options available) we will ship your order to your location. Basic delivery options are offered free of charge for orders over $300. Rush orders and expedited shipping can be applied during the checkout.
